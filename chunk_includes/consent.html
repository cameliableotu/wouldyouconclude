Purpose of the study

In this study, Adina Camelia Bleotu from ZAS Berlin and University of Bucharest, Anton Benz from ZAS Berlin and Roxana Mihaela PÄƒtrunjel from the University of Bucharest are investigating some linguistic phenomena in English. The information collected in this study will be used to prepare papers for linguistic conferences and journals. You are eligible to take part in this study because you are a native English speaker older than 18 years old.

Procedures

In this study, you will first have to answer some questions about your linguistic background, then you will have to pick the most natural interpretation of a sentence in a certain context and rate the acceptability of that sentence on a scale from 1 (totally unacceptable) to 7 (very acceptable). The whole process should last around 10-15 minutes. Please make sure that you have answered all the questions carefully. After completing the test, you will receive a code which you can enter on MTurk.

Risks

There are no risks in this experimental procedure. However, in online research, there is always a risk of a breach of confidentiality. In order to prevent this from happening, we will not be collecting information that uniquely identifies participants on the servers where our data is collected.

Benefits

Data confidentiality

Your answers will remain confidential. Your name will not appear in any paper or conference presentation. In order to ensure confidentiality, your answers will be associated with a number even within our own analyses.

Refusal to take part in or retreating from the study

Your participation in this study is purely voluntary. You do not have to provide information against your will. You can choose to give up at any point, but, in that case, you will not receive a reward.
